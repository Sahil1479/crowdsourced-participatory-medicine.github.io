    Purpose of this Study

    There is an enormous heterogeneity in clinical outcomes of COVID and methods are needed to aggregate phenotypes in long COVID. Since long COVID could extended for much longer periods after initial infection, it also becomes uncertain whether the patients disease states are due to precipitation of other comorbid conditions or a a natural history of COVID infection. The final aim is to evolve a data-science driven participatory medicine framework for aggregating information from experiences of millions of patients and family members of diverse demographic regions through a data-science driven participatory medicine framework .

    COVID Symptoms

    Although there is no common symptoms of COVID-19 infection and it has shown multiple effects on different persons. This study will help to analyze the symptoms shown by COVID19 on different individuals. Some of the common symptoms include

    Fever

    Fever is one of the most common symptoms of COVID 19.

    Dizziness

    Many person affected from COVID-19 complained about feeling dizziness.

    Loss of taste and smell

    People also reported loss of taste and smell while suffering from COVID-19.

    Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath

    In severe cases of COVID-19 infection people also faced difficulty in breathing or shortness of breathe.

    Chest Pain

    Many people reported chest pain during the infection.

    Loss of appetite

    People also faced loss of appetite due to COVID infection.

    Aim of this project

    The aim of the project is to understand determinants of COVID outcomes from experiences of millions of patients and family members of diverse demographic regions through a data-science driven participatory medicine framework

    • Development of data Science framework

      A data-science framework will be developed.

    • Evaluation of intrinsic and extrinsic factors of COVID 19.

      Some extrinsic and intrinsic factors will be evaluated.

    • Assessing the role of Peer learning and social media.

      In this project we will try to assess the role of peer learning, social media and behavioural patterns in patient choice and COVID outcomes

    • Participatory vs clinical setting

      Using this project we will assess the outcomes in participatory vs clinical settings

    Development of Data Science framework

    A data-science framework will be developed in the project.

    Data-science framework will be used to aggregate patient notes and experiences from crowdsourced structured and non-structured data.

    Evaluating extrinsic and intrinsic factors

    Effects of some intrinsic and extrinsic factors will be evaluated in the project.

    In this project we will try to evaluate the effect of intrinsic, extrinsic factors such as environmental, hygiene and social behaviour and cultural practices on outcomes

    Assessing the role of different medium.

    We will assess the role of different medium in this project.

    In this project we will try to assess the role of peer learning, social media and behavioural patterns in patient choice and COVID outcomes

    Participatory vs Clinical setting

    We will assess the outcomes in participatory vs clinical settings

    The outcomes in participatory vs clinical settings will be analyzed and using the results we can inform others to take actions accordingly.
